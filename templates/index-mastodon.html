    • 🦣 mastodon-archive

      Because the things we like shouldn't have to be lost forever
      See also: twitter-archive

      Do you remember that amazing piece of art you liked on Mastodon 2 months ago? Do you ever wonder if you'll see it again?

      This is an iOS shortcut that allows you to download and automatically organize the contents of a Mastodon post to iCloud.

      How To Use

      Once you've installed the shortcut it should appear in your share sheet when you go to share a link. Press the share button on a Mastodon post and select the shortcut from the list.


      The shortcut will then download the contents of the post and save it to iCloud in folders organised by the post authors name. You can then access it in the Files app. Text content along with the original URLs for the post are also saved in a separate folder.

      Files will be saved in:

      📁 iCloud Drive > Shortcuts > Mastodon Archive 📁 iCloud Drive > Shortcuts > Mastodon Archive (Text)
