      How To Use

      Once you've installed the shortcut it should appear in your share sheet when you share a link. To access it in the iOS Twitter app, hold down the share button and select the shortcut from the list.

      The shortcut will then download the contents of the tweet and save it to iCloud in folders organised by the tweet authors name. You can then access it in the Files app. Text content along with the original URL's for the tweet are also saved in a separate folder.
